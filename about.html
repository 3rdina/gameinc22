WHAT IS GAMEINC?

GameInc, which stands for Game Mini Carnival, is an event where we create the opportunity for the public to become beta testers for the games that were created by semester four students from MC111. With the feedback received from the beta testers, we shall improve our game and also, your gaming experience!

This is the logo of GAMEINC 2016 which is the very first time GAMEINC was held by MC111 students.
In this video, we can see how amazing and how interesting GAMEINC are. From this video we can see there are lots of fun activities there.

The games are produced are based on the catagories that given which is Adventure Game, Puzzle Game, Strategy Game, Hidden Object Game,
FPS Game, Card & Board Game, Arcade & Action Game and Words & Trivia Game

This is the logo of GAMEINC 2017 which is the second time GAMEINC was held. Same goes to GAMEINC 2016, there are lots of different types of games that are provided to anyone who wants to try games that are made by MC111 students.

The games are produced are based on the catagories that given which is Adventure Game, Puzzle Game, Strategy Game, Arcade & Action Game and Words & Trivia Game.

Also, GameInc 2017 has a mini tournament for Dota 2 players which is 1 vs 1 mode. The interesting thing is, there are no entrance fees for the players and there are prizes to be won.

GAMEINC18 used this logo for GAMEINC18 events.

The games are produced are based on the catagories that given which is Adventure Game, Puzzle Game, Strategy Game, Arcade & Action Game and Words & Trivia Game.

GameInc 2018 have prepared a mini esports tournament. The Tournaments that were prepared by GameInc are FIFA 18 and Mobile Legends Bang Bang. Winners can win prizes and it is very worth it.

The logo that GAMEINC19 used for GAMEINC19.

The games are produced are based on the catagories that given which is Game Storm, Puzzle Game, Strategy Game, Arcade Game, Action Game, Words & Trivia Game and Hidden Object Game.

Also, GAMEINC19 was sponsored by Hausboom, SPM Games, LHDNM, Pharmaniaga, Print Channel, Cadbury and JM Boutique Hotel Penang.

This is the logo for GAMEINC20. GAMEINC20 makes a difference then previous GAMEINC when the official theme for GAMEINC20 is Cyberpunk.

The logo for GAMEINC20 comes with a slogan “From Gamers To Creators”. The games are produced are based on the themes which is Cyberpunk.

For the first time ever, GAMEINC20 needs to be held online due to Covid-19 pandemic and to make sure everyone is safe. To test all of the games from GAMEINC20, students need to download the games from Itch.io. Students can evaluate the games at Itch.io also.

In order to maintain the identity, the same logo from GAMEINC20 was used for GAMEINC21. For the 2nd time, GAMEINC21 is held online as well due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

The theme for GAMEINC21 is "Futuristic Space" “Gaming Beyond Reality”. The games are produced are based on the themes which is "Educational Games".
