In Sheo’s Shop Thru, you play as Sheo, a little robot boy tasked by his mother to help collect ingredients for making a 3-course-meal. Unfortunately, as Sheo goes along the game, he has to avoid being stopped by the robot Karens as they want to take Sheo’s ingredients for their own selfish gain. Using his abilities, Sheo must overcome this obstacle to make sure he arrives to his mother on time, with the meal saved for his family’s delight!. Users can also learn Sheo’s cooking recipe for each dish by the end of the level as a reward.
